Likes and Interests

Music

I've loved music since I got my first walkman in middleschool and have continued to have passion for it ever since. Some of my favorite bands and musicians are The Grateful Dead, Pearl Jam, Weezer, Made in Heights, The Smashing Pumpkins, Bassnectar, Zed's Dead, Rusko and all things Deep House and House.

Meditation

I started practicing mindfulness meditation in college and it has greatly influenced my life. I currently use the app headspace to practice, and I try my best to be as present as possible in everything that I do. I strongly recommend it to anyone looking for a better sense of balance, peace and understanding in their day to day activities and relationships.
